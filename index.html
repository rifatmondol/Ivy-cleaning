How are we prepared for Covid-19?

  • We are certified by the Global Biorisk Advisory Committee (GBAC) in cleaning for Covid
  • Staff has been trained in proper CDC protocol and has taken classes via the CDC and the Red Cross in sanitizing, PPE, and proper hand-washing
  • All equipment is sanitized in between jobs
  • We practice distancing by asking that clients are either not home or remain in different areas of the house during cleanings
  • All cleaners wear masks and gloves
  • Client can choose to add steaming or disinfecting via EPA registered agents for emerging pathogens
  • Techs cannot report to work if they are unwell
